Get Involved

The Southwest Manor Civic Club runs on neighbor power. Whether you have a few hours a month or can help with a single event, your time and skills make a difference.

Ways to Participate

  • Membership: Support our projects with annual dues of $120.00 per household.
  • Event Volunteer: Help set up, greet neighbors, or serve refreshments at meetings and clean-ups.
  • Beautification Team: Join seasonal projects like planting, litter patrol, and park maintenance.
  • Neighborhood Watch: Collaborate with local law enforcement to promote safety.

Sign Up

Fill out the volunteer form below or email us at volunteer@southwestmanorcivicclub.org. Someone from our team will follow up with details and schedules.

Donate

Not enough time to volunteer? Consider a tax-deductible donation to fund landscaping, signage, and community programs.
