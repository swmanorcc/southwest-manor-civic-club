About Us

Who We Are

The Southwest Manor Civic Club is a non-profit, resident-led group formed to maintain and improve the quality of life for everyone in our neighborhood. We serve as a voice for residents, coordinating with city departments and local leaders to address concerns such as beautification, safety, and community engagement.

What We Do

  • Organize regular meetings and neighborhood events.
  • Communicate important city updates and services.
  • Coordinate beautification projects and clean-ups.
  • Advocate on behalf of residents with local government.
