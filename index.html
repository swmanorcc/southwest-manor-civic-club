Welcome to Southwest Manor Civic Club

The Southwest Manor Civic Club is a volunteer neighborhood organization dedicated to keeping our community safe, clean, and connected. We work with residents, city officials, and local partners to promote pride in our neighborhood and address issues that matter to you.

Our Mission

To foster a clean, attractive, and safe environment where neighbors can thrive, stay informed, and build lasting connections.
